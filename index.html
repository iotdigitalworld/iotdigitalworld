About

The growing emphasis on digital transformation is encouraging more organizations to adopt initiatives driven by the Internet of Things (IoT). IoT is creating a giant network where all the devices are connected to each other and providing them with the capability to interact with each other. This is driving the automation to a next level where devices will communicate with each other and make decisions on their own without any human interventions. IOT Digital World is the platform created by the Professors and Students of Shah and Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College, which will help you understand the different aspects of Internet of things in form of multiple real world projects, Tutorials and research papers. This website is not only helpful for the students but also enthusiastic learners seeking help to understand the concepts of IOT in detail and stay up to date with the latest developments in IOT. Most importantly, it will provide learners with greater intelligence and real-time operational insight of the current technologies.
